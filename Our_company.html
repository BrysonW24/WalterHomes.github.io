Our Company

Walter Homes, a family business with a heritage of building excellence since 1978, constructing enduring homes of quality and style tailored to their client's individual needs.

Walter Homes

Walter Homes Pty. Ltd. have an excellent reputation in the building industry for constructing high quality, individually designed and custom built homes.

Walter Homes Pty. Ltd. offer our clients personalised service and high standards of workmanship from start to finish, at all times endeavouring to make the construction of your new home an exciting and enjoyable experience.

Our dedication to achieving high quality results has rewarded both Walter Homes and their clients with numerous housing awards from both the Master Builders Association (M.B.A.) and the Housing Industry Association (H.I.A.) since 1990.

To maintain our reputation, our homes are built and finished by our skilled tradesmen, the cornerstone of our business, under the direct supervision of Brett Walter, this is to assure you that your new home will be of the highest standard and finish to suit your budget. Our philosophy is "we strive to achieve excellence at all times".

Our Team

We have a team of skilled professionals happy to assist you in your design and decision making:- architect/draftsman, engineers, interior decorator/colour consultant, estimator and builders. This ensures your building experience is always under the control of a highly experienced and motivated team of people totally committed to you and your investment.

Walter Homes focuses on building it's homes to these award-winning standards, because a satisfied client is our best form of advertising. Once you step inside a Walter Homes house, you enter a world of unsurpassed craftsmanship and style.

We specialise in full brick and or concrete slab, brick Veneer, sloping and or narrow blocks, bearer and joists.

Areas Served
Sydney's North Shore, Northern Districts, The Hills, Hornsby, Strathfield, Concord, Drummoyne, Woolwich, Hunters Hill, Greenwich and neighbouring districts.
