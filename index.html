ABOUT US

It’s been 40 years since Walter Homes began revolutionising home building and after all this time we’re still recognised as industry Master Builders. That’s because we’re always looking to the future, continuing to harness our knowledge and resources to take home building to the next level. We’re constantly investing in cutting edge building technologies and materials, while at the same time, reinvigorating the market with innovative home design.

DELIVERING BEAUTIFUL HOMES
Even after 40 years, Walter Homes is still highly awarded as one of NSW’s top builders. This is because at our heart, we’ve always been committed to delivering homes which are beautiful as well as functional and affordable. Walter Homes is proud to help Australians achieve their dream of owning their own home.
