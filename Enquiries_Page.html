NEW ENQUIRIES

Walter Homes has always understood that a home is more than just the components we use in the building process. It’s a place where we grow and develop relationships, nurture the families dreams we help to create with memories to last a lifetime.

We’re committed to this ideology and it’s one that will see us continuing to design and build tomorrow’s homes today.

Ask a Walter Home's team member how we can help.

Book an appointment with an expert consultant at one of our display villages so you can get help deciding which amazing design or home and land package is best for you.

Want to talk face to face with a real person but can’t make it to us? No problem! Let us know the basics of your ideas and we will get in touch and come to you. 

Don’t forget to ask about our free site inspections.
