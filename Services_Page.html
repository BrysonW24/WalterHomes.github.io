Services

What you can expect when building your dream home with Walter Homes Pty Ltd

Walter homes has a range of services on offer which can be utilised as a whole or individually to aid in the design, construction and the fit-out of your newly built home.

Here to Support out Clients

We can give you an obligation free site inspection and assessment of your property. 

Walter homes has a design team which will meet with you to discuss, listen and advise with you on your design, construction and lifestyle requirements. The designs which they will present to you will incorporate the latest building techniques and materials.

Product Sourcing

We have made strong connections to our suppliers over the years, and becuase of that we can help you save! Walter Homes will source you the products for your home whether it be a fridge, TV, carpets, flooring or furniture at an extremely competitive rate that will keep money in your pocket.

Unique Styles

We'll take control of the whole process of submitting your plans to council once your engineering, landscape and architectural plans have been finalised to your satisfaction.

Might need a decorator? That's something we can make available to you - a decorator and colour consultant to assist in your decision making in this area.

Hand picked team

Our skilful team of Master craftsmen are hand selected to ensure each of our quality new homes are constructed to our exacting requirements

Not to mention a design service for you too. 


Stress Free process from start to finish

We deal with all the relevent authorities on the clients behalf in processing the building application.

38 years of building experience, personalised service & daily site supervision.

Unique Styles

A complete building service from concept to completion. Custom design service for all of our customers all the way through the building process for your satisfaction.

We can arrange for surveying, service disconnections and connections, and council requirements on the block of land for you as there may be design and physical restrictions on the building of your future home upon it.
