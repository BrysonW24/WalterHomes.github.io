WALTER HOMES SERVICES & INCLUSIONS

Heritage Homes


In some areas of Sydney's Northern suburbs within certain councils there are heritage areas. In these areas therefore the home to be built requires meeting strict architectural heritage guidelines.

Walter Homes has the expertise to design and build you a modern home with all the character of yesteryear while adhering to strict council regulations. Walter Homes has extensive knowledge of related heritage issues with councils in the North Shore of Sydney.

Redeveloping


With a trend towards medium-sized blocks in some areas of Sydney, a growing percentage of people with properties in older, established suburbs are looking to redevelop or subdivide their block.

You may have bought your property to buy into the area and now want to construct the home of your dreams on this block, or you may just want to update your current address to create a modern home with all the luxuries of today's living.

Thinking about a Knockdown Rebuild?

Love your address but not your home? We have the answer.

Do you dream of living in a new, modern home with plenty of customised finishes? Whether you're looking to knock down and rebuild your old home, or have purchased a property to redevelop, we can help!

We’ll help you organise the demolition of your home and will recommend a demolition partner to ensure you feel confident during the knock down phase of your project.

Dual Occupancy & Duplex 

If you are looking for ways to maximise the potential of your land or seeking an investment opportunity, you should consider building a duplex home.

Essentially a duplex home consists of two separate living quarters and entrances that can cater for two different households, all within one dwelling. Duplexes can be the perfect living arrangement and create substantial financial gain and investment potential.

Designed perfectly for creating a stylish combination of both primary residence and an investment property or just a residence for a larger family, please enquire if you wish to discuss further.
